DD4 AI use policy
=================

DD4 has an official AI use policy for game development.  It is as follows:

*   The core MUD codebase should remain human-led and human-understood.

*   AI can assist, but should not autonomously design, restructure, or
    mass-implement core game systems.

*   All core code changes should be small enough that a human contributor can
    explain, review, and maintain them.

*   Agentic development is fine for adjacent tools, clients, websites, builders,
    and experiments, but preferably these live in separate repos.

*   If we ever want to revise this policy later, we will do so deliberately
    rather than drifting into practices that do not align with these principles.

Signed off by Brutus, Gezhp, Owl, and Shade, 11/6/26

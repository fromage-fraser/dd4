[1.1]  What flavours of this FAQ are there and how can I get them?

Currently, this FAQ is available only in HTML format. It can be browsed through and downloaded from the DD website [see FAQ 1.2].

[1.2]  Does this MUD have a website?

The MUD has a website at www.dragons-domain.org. A great deal of information is posted there, and all players are encouraged to check it out.

[1.3]  How can I get someone to help me or answer a question?

If you have a problem, then check this FAQ, the online help files (try typing HELP for starters) and the website documentation. If online, you may ask for help from other players, but don't be surprised if they tell you "check the website" or "read the FAQ". If you're really stuck you should write a note to the Immortals (see "HELP NOTE"). Players are generally friendly though, so don't be too afraid to ask (politely, of course!).
